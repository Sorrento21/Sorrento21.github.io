About

Welcome to my world

Hi! This is Jiacheng Liu (Jason) speaking.

I’m a Tsinghua University student majoring in Creative Design and Intelligent Engineering (CDIE), a cross-disciplinary field within the realm of automation. My coursework in automation, machinery, and design has cultivated a broad academic perspective and fostered my creativity.

I’ve excelled in my major studies, maintaining a GPA of 3.96 out of 4.00. Notably, I earned A+ grades in Design Thinking, Principles of Circuits, and Calculus B(2).

Beyond academics, I am a passionate researcher. Since my second year of college, I’ve been immersed in research, focusing on smart home environments, VR experiences, and human-computer interaction involving olfactory sensation.

My research interest lies in HCI(human-computer interaction). I am currently involved in the research of PPG (Photoplethysmogram) signals, or one could say, the field of affective computing, in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University. In the future, I also aspire to explore the field of ubiquitous computing and wearable sensing.

You can find more information about me in my CV.

Hobby

Mixing

In addition to my studies, I am an amateur mixologist! I’ve transformed my dormitory into a cozy little bar to unwind during leisure time, where I craft my own cocktails. I’ve recreated classic cocktails such as Tequila Sunrise, Passionate Coast, Mojito, Long Island Iced Tea, and Godfather. Occasionally, I experiment with crafting my own cocktails by mixing various syrups, liqueurs, and base spirits!

Painting

I am also an art enthusiast. During my time in the department, I served as the Publicity Department Head for a student organization, creating numerous paintings, cultural and creative products, promotional materials, and posters. In my spare time, I continue to produce my own artworks, exploring various mediums such as watercolor, gouache, sketching, impasto, vector art, and Chinese ink painting.

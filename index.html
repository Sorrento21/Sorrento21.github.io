Hi, I'm Jiacheng Liu 刘佳成

I am a senior student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Creative Design and Intelligent Engineering (CDIE)—an interdisciplinary program that integrates electrical engineering, computer science, mechanical engineering, and industrial design. My passion lies in exploring the convergence of artificial intelligence and sensing systems to develop early detection solutions and assistive devices that offer seamless, unobtrusive health support for individuals with diverse medical needs. I aspire to contribute to a future where AI-assisted healthcare technologies empower both the general public and medical professionals.

This year, I will earn my Bachelor of Engineering degree from the CDIE program at Xinya College, Tsinghua University. I have gained valuable research experience in multiple esteemed labs, including the PI Lab and the Future Lab at Tsinghua University, as well as the SciFi Lab at Cornell University.

Research Interests

How can we integrate AI with sensing systems to develop early detection systems and assistive devices for everyday use, providing unobtrusive and seamless health support for individuals with diverse health conditions?

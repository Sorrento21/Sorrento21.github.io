Painting

I am an art enthusiast. During my time in the department, I served as Head of the Publicity Department for a student organization, where I created numerous paintings, cultural and creative products, promotional materials, and posters. In my spare time, I continue to create my own artwork, experimenting with various mediums such as watercolor, gouache, sketching, impasto, vector art, and Chinese ink painting.

Mixing

Apart from my studies, I'm an amateur bartender for my friends! I've turned my dorm room into a cozy little bar where I can relax and create my own cocktails. I've mastered classics like Tequila Sunrise, Passionate Coast, Mojito, Long Island Iced Tea, and Godfather. From time to time, I also enjoy experimenting with new recipes by mixing different syrups, liqueurs, and base spirits!

Tequila Sunrise

Tequila, Orange Juice, Grenadine

This cocktail has a gradient color effect that looks like a sunrise, with orange juice at the top and a redder hue at the bottom from the grenadine.
